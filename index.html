M3M and Jacob & Co Vision Turns into a Luxury Landmark. When two giants unite, something extraordinary emerges. M3M and Jacob & Co are rewriting Noida's skyline with pure brilliance. This collaboration blends architectural mastery with the unmatched artistry of a global luxury icon. Every corner speaks of innovation, exclusivity, and timeless sophistication.

Set in Sector 97, Noida, the project redefines what premium living truly means. The high-rise towers stand as symbols of ambition, designed for those who expect more from life. Every façade glows with refined detail, capturing the signature essence of Jacob & Co.'s perfection in creation. It's not just construction; it's creation designed to inspire awe at every glance.

The location offers flawless connectivity to everything that matters. The Greater Noida Expressway links you to vibrant business districts, entertainment hubs, and the upcoming Jewar Airport. From Amity University to Okhla Bird Sanctuary, every destination feels effortlessly accessible. Convenience meets class in a setting that feels both urban and exclusive.

Inside, the homes exude calm, comfort, and charm. The project is set to offer an exclusive range of 3BHK, 4BHK, and 5BHK luxury residences, with prices starting around ₹7.5 crore and going up to ₹25 crore. In its first phase, the development will introduce around 150 premium homes designed for elegant living. The second phase will follow with nearly 100 ultra-luxury serviced residences, offering a curated lifestyle experience. The entire project is anticipated to be completed within the next three years, marking a new benchmark in high-end urban living.

The lifestyle here is beyond expectation. Residents enjoy a resort-inspired pool, meditation zones, a modern gym, and a vibrant clubhouse. Lush green parks, a mini theater, and designer lounges complete the experience. Every space celebrates balance, where peace and pleasure live side by side. Their partnership marks a turning point for luxury real estate in India. It's not just about homes; it's about heritage, innovation, and passion captured in architecture. This is where iconic design meets limitless imagination.
